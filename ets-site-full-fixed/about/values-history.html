Values and history

History

From Rome roots to global workflows.

Born in Rome, ETS supported Italian broadcasters and international studios for decades, growing from dubbing into full audio post and rights support. Our studios and partner network evolved with the industry—from linear TV to streaming platforms.

Today, ETS Group connects localisation, production, distribution and licensing/publishing under one roof so creative intent, schedules and rights stay aligned across every territory.

Values

Quality first

Respect for the original and excellence in every local version.

Reliability

Clear schedules, transparent communication, on‑time delivery.

Security

Responsible handling of materials, rights and approvals.

People

Directors, actors and engineers who love the craft.

Partnership

We solve problems shoulder‑to‑shoulder with our clients.
